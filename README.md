# Amazon Sales Performance Dashboard

### Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze Amazon sales performance and support data-driven business decisions. The dashboard evaluates overall business health, identifies key revenue drivers, and highlights opportunities for revenue growth and profitability optimization using dynamic KPIs, charts, and slicers.

### Business Problem
How is the Amazon business performing, what drives sales, and where are the opportunities to increase revenue and reduce losses?

### Data Source: Kaggle
The dataset was pre-cleaned. Data validation checks and necessary transformations were performed to support time-based, product-level, and category-level analysis.

### Tools & Techniques Used

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts

GETPIVOTDATA for dynamic KPI calculations

Slicers for interactive filtering

Calculated metrics (Revenue, Profit, AOV)

Dashboard layout and visual storytelling

### Key Metrics (KPIs)

Total Orders – Overall sales volume

Total Net Revenue – Total sales value generated

Total Profit – Business profitability

Average Order Value (AOV) – Revenue per customer transaction

All KPIs dynamically update based on slicer selections.

### Key Insights

* Revenue is driven by a relatively small number of top-performing products.

* Sales performance remains generally stable across months, with identifiable stronger periods.

* Certain categories consistently outperform others, indicating uneven revenue contribution.

* Average Order Value is stable, suggesting growth opportunities through customer upselling.

### Business Recommendations

* Scale top-selling products by increasing inventory, advertising, and visibility.

* Increase Average Order Value through bundling and cross-selling strategies.

* Focus investment on high-performing categories to maximize returns.

* Align promotions and inventory planning with stronger sales months.

* Prioritize high-performing countries for targeted marketing and logistics optimization.

### Business Value

This dashboard enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor sales and profitability performance at a glance

* Identify key revenue drivers across products, categories, and regions

* Make informed decisions on inventory, pricing, and marketing strategy

* Support sustainable revenue growth and margin optimization
